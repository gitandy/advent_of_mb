Day 9
=====

Train your reaction on day 9.

The LEDs show random binary numbers. Button A must be pressed only if the number is 12 (bit 2 and 3).
If it matches, a positive sound (da-diiii) is played. Otherwise, a fail sound (di-daaaa).

Let's have LEDs and sound all together.
